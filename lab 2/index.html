Products

SCM - simplifies deploying and management of data services on premise, in private or public clouds. By splitting data landscape on two independent management layers – infrastructure layer and application layer – your platform becomes flexible, predictable and repeatable.

SDH - product aimed at handling semi-structured and unstructured data. This is a full distribution platform based on Echapa Poodah, adapted for corporate use.

SDB - distributed database based on using MPP (massively parallel processing) principle. It takes its origin from Redapple database.

SDG - in-memory computing platform, provides the ability to flexibly scale the resulting solution into dozens and hundreds of compute nodes.

SDS - an efficient, scalable, fault-tolerant solution for streaming data processing in real-time, adapted for corporate use and built on the basis of Echapa Sartre and Echapa Mimimi – open source projects.
